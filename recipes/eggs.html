Egg Recipe

Description

There are multiple ways to cook an egg. It's up to you on how you want your egg to taste. This recipe will instruct you on how to fully cook your egg for your meal.

Ingredients:

  • Oil
  • Egg(s)

Steps:

  • Grab the amount of eggs you want and crack them into a bowl, then mix for about 1-2 minutes.
  • Heat up your pan with your choice of oil and dump your beat up eggs into the pan after 1-2 minutes.
  • Wait until the egg whites become solid white, then flip the egg over and repeat this process for the yolk
  • Your eggs are ready after a few minutes of waiting!
