Simple Salad Recipe

Description:

Salad is the most refreshing food to be eaten at anytime of the year. Salads provide an innumerable amount of nutritions for your body to stay healthy.

Ingredients:

  • Any sort of lettuce of your choice
  • Ranch
  • 1 full lemon
  • 5 large tomatoes
  • 20 sliced olives (or more)
  • Shredded cheese of your choice
  • Any sort of nuts
  • Raisins

    • Steps:

  • Wash your lettuce and let them dry for a while.
  • In the meantime, slice your tomatoes,olives, and lemon.
  • After your lettuce has dried, combine your lettuce with your sliced toppings and add however much ranch you desire.
  • Begin mixing until everything is evenly spread out, and enjoy!
